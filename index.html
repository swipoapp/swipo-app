How it works

Three swipes to a clean gallery

No complicated menus. No endless scrolling. Just swipe each photo to a corner and let Swipo handle the rest.

Step 1

Swipe to any corner

Each corner means something different — delete, keep, skip, or favorite. It's intuitive from the first swipe.

Step 2

Review your decisions

Before anything is deleted, review every choice. Change your mind? Just move photos between piles.

Step 3

Done. Space freed.

Confirm and Swipo cleans up. Deleted photos go to Recently Deleted first — you have 30 days to recover them.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Powerful features that stay out of your way. Clean your library faster than you thought possible.

Smart Decks

On-device AI groups your photos by type — screenshots, selfies, food, landscapes — so you can clean one category at a time.

Clean by Year

Time travel through your gallery. Pick any year and start sorting — perfect for tackling that 2019 backlog.

Trip Cleaner

Sort photos by location clusters. Went overboard on vacation snaps? Clean up trip by trip, keep the best ones.

Custom Filters

Filter by date range, photo count, or media type. Build your own sorting sessions exactly how you want them.

30-Day Safety Net

Nothing disappears instantly. All deleted photos go to Recently Deleted first, giving you 30 days to change your mind.

No Subscription

Pay once, own forever. No monthly fees, no annual renewals, no "premium tier" upsells. $3.99 and it's yours.
